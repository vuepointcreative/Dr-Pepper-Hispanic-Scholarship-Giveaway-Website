The SABEResPODER & Dr Pepper Hispanic Scholarship is for Latinx students that want to get a college education so that they can achieve their goals, and give back to their families, and communities.

  • Must be at least 18 years of age or older on 12/5/2016
  • Live in the United States (residents of New York or Florida are not eligible for this scholarship)
  • Must be able to provide a social security number or tax identification number if prize is awarded
  • Must be currently enrolled or will be enrolled by Fall 2017 in a full-time associate’s or bachelor’s degree program.
