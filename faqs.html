FAQs

How do I apply?

Please visit our Rules Page for instructions on how to apply.

When is the last day to apply?

You must upload a video to your Facebook page, and submit a complete application, by December 31, 2016 by 11:59 p.m. Pacific Standard Time.

How do I know if SABEResPODER received my application?

You will receive a confirmation page once you submit your application. Be sure to keep a copy of this page for your records. If you do NOT receive this confirmation page, contact SABEResPODER via email at scholarship@saberespoder.com

Is there a fee to apply?

No.

How will semi-finalists be selected?

Our Scholarship Award Committee will select semi-finalists based on:

  • Content and quality of writing sample
  • Content and quality of Facebook video
  • Number of likes / shares on Facebook

When will semi-finalists be notified?

Semi-finalists will be notified on January 23, 2017 via text message, and announced on the program website. Further instructions will be provided at that time.

When does the semi-final round end?

The semi-final round closes on February 10, 2017.

What is the amount of the scholarship?

SABEResPODER and Dr Pepper will award 20 scholarships. Each scholarship will be in the amount of $2,500.

What if I win a scholarship, but I don’t have an ITIN or social security number?

You will need to have an ITIN under your name prior to receiving the award. Scholarship cannot be issued without a SSN or ITIN.
